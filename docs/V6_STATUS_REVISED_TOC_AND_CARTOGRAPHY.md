## PART 3 - REVISED THEORY OF CHANGE (reflects the actual ask; supersedes legacy 44204057)
The legacy ToC was about the abstract programme. THIS one is about the ASK: shipping a
DEIA-enabled Observatory & Barometer that survives scrutiny.

PROBLEM: Data systems fail people they cannot see. v3 proved the idea was compelling but
was a rough mock-up of made-up numbers - it could not survive scrutiny or be acted on.

IF (inputs/activities):
- We overhaul v3 into a DEIA-enabled Observatory & Barometer, AND
- every figure carries an honest provenance flag (FACTUAL/MODELLED/ILLUSTRATIVE/PARTIAL + source), AND
- the system declares its own blind spots (Absent/Partial/Evidenced) rather than hiding them, AND
- the Barometer scores data-sharing maturity AND equity, disaggregated by population, AND
- a 360 accountability loop proves whether action actually worked for the affected cohort

THEN (outputs): a clickable, honest, evidence-grounded prototype that shows the real shape
of the thing - not made-up numbers - with its own gaps on the record.

WHICH LEADS TO (outcomes): an audience who engaged with the rough PoC can now interrogate a
credible artefact; decision-makers see where systems fail people and can prioritise by equity
risk; the programme earns the legitimacy to engage affected communities (gated on ART004).

ULTIMATELY (impact): data-sharing decisions that close equity gaps instead of widening them -
because the invisible were made visible and action was held to account.

FALSIFIERS (how we would know it failed):
- L1: if the overhaul reads as v3-with-extras, not a different class of thing -> failed.
- L2: if any figure cannot be traced to a named source -> evidence chain broken.
- L3: if blind spots are hidden rather than declared -> reproduces the original harm.
- L4: if the Barometer is display-only tiles, not a real scored model -> not a barometer.
- L5: if the loop has no return path (no proof action worked for the cohort) -> just a dashboard.
- L6 (Goodhart): if Health Equity Score becomes a target gamed rather than a true signal -> failed.

KEY ASSUMPTION HELD HONESTLY: community 'with/by/for' claims are CONTINGENT until ART004 is
complete AND real engagement has happened. Until then, lived experience is represented by
composite personas flagged ILLUSTRATIVE - never claimed as validated.
